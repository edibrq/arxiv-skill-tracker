### 2. Mem²Evolve: Towards Self-Evolving Agents via Co-Evolutionary Capability Expansion and Experience Distillation

**arXiv:** [2604.10923](https://arxiv.org/abs/2604.10923)  
**Published:** 2026-04-13  
**Authors:** Zihao Cheng, Zeming Liu, Yingyu Shan, Xinyi Wang, Xiangrong Zhu, Yunpu Ma, Hongru Wang, Yuhang Guo, Wei Lin, Yunhong Wang

### Abstract
Existing self-evolving frameworks treat experience accumulation and asset creation in isolation. Mem²Evolve introduces co-evolutionary Capability Expansion and Experience Distillation with two core components:
- **Experience Memory** - accumulated experience to guide asset creation
- **Asset Memory** - dynamic creation of tools/expert agents

Results: 18.53% improvement over standard LLMs, 11.80% over experience-only evolution, 6.46% over asset-only evolution across 6 task categories and 8 benchmarks.
